Cyber Safety Awareness

Stay safe online by learning how to protect your digital life.

  1. What is Cyber Safety?

    2. Cyber safety refers to protecting your personal information and devices from cyber threats. With the increasing use of the internet, cyber safety has become essential for everyone.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing attacks
  • Malware and viruses
  • Identity theft
  • Online scams

Tips to Stay Safe Online

  • Use strong passwords and update them regularly
  • Be cautious while clicking on links or downloading attachments
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep your software and antivirus updated
